About Me

Outside of school, I like to stay busy with physical activity, clubs, personal projects, and social events. My favorite sport by far is climbing, and I love going out and climbing in nature! In addition to climbing, I've been golfing my whole life and I've recently started playing tennis. Staying active is very important to me, and it allows me to destress after a hard day's work.

As far as personal projects, I often have a couple of things that I'm working on in my free time. At the moment, I have been occupying myself with building a personal website to host my resume, contact info, and personal overview. It's been a lot of fun to learn HTML and CSS through research, trial, and error! My other recent, much bigger personal endeavor was a project I undertook with a couple of friends. Together, we built an Android app that used real-time data to tell a user the best parking spot available based on the lot's population and the spot's proximity to the user's desired building. Without any prior exposure to Android Studio or OpenCV, the three of us built the app and used it successfully with a model lot and webcam. It was a lot of fun, and we plan on continuing development in the future!
